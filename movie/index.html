Put On a Happy Face.

For Better or Worse, Superhero Movies Will Never Be the Same.

Joker is a movie about a homicidal narcissist who feels entitled to the world’s attention —
a man who’d rather kill for a good laugh than allow the world to treat him like its punchline.
It’s also a movie about the dehumanizing effects of a capitalistic system that greases the economic ladder,
blurring the line between private wealth and personal worth until life itself loses its absolute value.
